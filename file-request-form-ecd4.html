File Request Form

Upon submitting the form, you will automatically receive an email after 5 to 10 seconds with the link of the file. If you still have not receive an email, please check if that email exists or active, or check also for any typos and unnecessary spaces, and then try again later.

It is sad to say that almost half of the requests were not able to receive the file because they are not using their DepEd emails. We are using AUTOMATION in replying quickly to your requests, that means you receive an auto-reply from our system and the messages that system sent are based on the conditions we set. The safest way for our system to detect legitimate account , misrepresented accounts or fake accounts , and to avoid intruders and spammers is through DepEd Emails. So, your workaround would be to ask a favor to a teacher whom you know with the DepEd email to download the file for you. I believe that DepEd email is required for all DepEd Teachers and you should have one.
